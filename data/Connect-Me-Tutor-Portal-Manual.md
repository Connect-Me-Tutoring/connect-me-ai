# **Tutor Portal Sign Up**

1. You should have received a sign up link in your inbox or spam within 1-2 days of joining the Discord. If you did not receive an email to register on the portal, please contact a Connect Me Board member on Remind or Discord.

# **Forgot Password**

1. **Resetting your password**  
   1. In the login page locate and click on “**Forgot Password?**”. You will be redirected to a form requesting your email address to your account.   
   2. Once you have entered your email, you will receive a 6-digit code through your email that you will need to re-enter back into the form.   
   3. Once the portal has received the correct 6-digit code, you will be rerouted to a page to set a new password.

**Announcements:** This channel is where the admin will send any important announcements\!

**My Students:** This is where you can view information about your student.

**My Enrollments:** This is where you can edit the time of your sessions and create additional sessions. 

* To edit the session press:         
  * Choose another session time within the student’s availability and find an open zoom link. If you can’t select a time, please contact Claudia Wang, Addison Duong, or Yulianna Kowalczyk.   
* If the student no longer wants tutoring, please **notify Yulianna** and **delete the enrollment.**  
* If you and your student would like to do more sessions weekly, add a new enrollment. **Green dot** \= zoom link available. **Red dot** \= unavailable.  
* If you are having trouble finding an open zoom link, please contact Claudia Wang, Addison Duong, or Yulianna Kowalczyk.   
* If you are only adding a **temporary session** for that week, please make sure to **delete the enrollment** after filling out the SEF\!
